Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Gena a girl?

Kirke's A: Not quiet, and any confusion wasn't on purpose. That's just what happens when you combine historical accuracy and androgyny.

Jojo's A: Nope! He is our beloved little twink although in the design phase we definitely intended for it to confuse people.

Vita’s A: One of them is lying.

Q: What are the goals with historical accuracy?

Kirke's A: Design wise, I really aim to be more accurate but simplified for ease of redrawing it. The main thing I differ is more modern hair, taking what can best be described as a C-Drama approach. My goal is that you should be able to tell a lot about a character from their wardrobe, be that where they're from, where they've spent time in, their wealth and occupation, et cetera. Writting wise, I keep the dialogue loose and a mix of modern and older things. (I like to think we're translating the dialogue in a way.) I've done a lot of research to make sure there's a high level of accuracy aside from that. It's not perfect as I'm not a historian but I am trying.

Jojo's A: We are certainly trying to be accurate to a degree especially in regards to setting and the like although when it comes to dialogue it's a mix like Kirke said, essentially a healthy balance.

Vita’s A: Like Kirke and Jojo said, we aim for a nice blend of historical accuracy with some modern touches here and there. When recommending music, I look for aesthetics and lyrics that can emulate the time period without sounding too out of place.
