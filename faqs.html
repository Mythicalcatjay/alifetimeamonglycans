Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Gena a girl?

Kirke's A: Nope! Luckily, you are far from the first to think that so don't take it bad.

Jojo's A: Nope! He is our beloved little twink although in the design phase we definitely intended for it to confuse people.

Q: What are the goals with historical accuracy?

Kirke's A: Design wise, I really aim to be more accurate but simplified for ease of redrawing it. The main thing I differ is more modern hair, taking what can best be described as a C-Drama approach. My goal is that you should be able to tell a lot about a character from their wardrobe, be that where they're from, where they've spent time in, their wealth and occupation, et cetera. Writting wise, I keep the dialogue loose and a mix of modern and older things. (I like to think we're translating the dialogue in a way.) I've done a lot of research to make sure there's a high level of accuracy aside from that from that. It's not perfect as I'm not a historian but I am trying.

Jojo's A: We are certainly trying to be accurate to a degree especially in regards to setting and the like although when it comes to dialogue it's a mix like Kirke said, essentially a healthy balance.

Q: This website is working poorly for me, what can I use to fix it?

Kirke's A: This tends to happen if you're using Opera GX. Try using it in a different browser, Firefox, Chrome, Edge, and similar browsers should all work. I personally reccommend Firefox.

Jojo's A: As someone who uses Opera, I totally get it but Chrome and Firefox totally work!
