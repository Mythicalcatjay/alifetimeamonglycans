Gattina

Gattina is a flaxen chestnut mare who works as a cart horse. She's considered to be a pretty nice horse just by looking a her but she's not of any exceptional quality nor a pedigree. She's not the brightest nor the most experienced so she is prone to put herself into situaions, be that chasing birds to trying bite children's fingers. Still, she's never outright malicious with her actions and is damn good at her job so no one's going to complain about this not-so-trusty steed.
